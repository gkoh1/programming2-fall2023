So, you want to play Blackjack, huh? Well, in case you need a reminder, here are the rules:

  • You start with two cards, hit draws another card
  • The dealer starts with two cards, one face down.
  • Once you stand, the dealer hits until their score is at least 17
  • Highest score under 22 wins. Dealer wins ties unless both players get 21, in which case it is a tie.
  • If you go over 21 (bust), you lose and the dealer doesn't play.
  • After the game is over, click reset to play again (you have no other choice . . .)
